three.jsAsync Compilation
Compare asyncCompilation: true against the default synchronous compilation.
Use spawn wave (streaming new assets) and rebuild all materials (shader editing) and watch the frame graph:
synchronous compilation freezes the loop (red bars), async compilation keeps every frame on budget —
changed objects keep their last material until the replacement is ready, new objects pop in like textures.
The center mesh sets compilePriority, so on a rebuild its new material lands before the grid's.
